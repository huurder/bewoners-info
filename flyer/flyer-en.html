Dear neighbour,

Let’s make our voice heard together.

Service costs, rent increases and repairs are easier to question when tenants are connected. This initiative helps Barnstijn Beheer tenants find each other and organize constructively.

bewoners-info.nl

You are receiving this letter because you may be a tenant of Barnstijn Beheer B.V. Many of us deal with the same questions: are service costs properly explained, are rent increases reasonable, and are needed repairs handled in time?

Individually, it is easy to feel small. Together, tenants can ask better questions, compare information and work toward a formal tenant committee.

Please visit bewoners-info.nl and fill in the short survey. Your answers help us understand how many tenants are willing to stay informed, compare service-cost information, or support a tenant committee.

Check service costs

Landlords must provide a clear annual breakdown of service costs. Comparing information helps tenants spot patterns.

Check the rent

The Huurcommissie rent-price check can help tenants understand whether a rent level is reasonable.

Build a committee

A tenant committee gives tenants a stronger formal position in conversations with the landlord.

This is a tenant-led initiative to help Barnstijn Beheer tenants find each other, compare concerns and decide what we can do together.
